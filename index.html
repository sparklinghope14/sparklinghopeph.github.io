We are youth. We are action. We are Sparkling Hope.

Sparkling Hope PH is a youth-led organization from Cagayan focused on environment, education, and community outreach. Join our programs, volunteer, or support our campaigns.

About Us

Who we are

A youth organization that builds community programs for health, education, and the environment. We lead tree plantings, feeding drives, and learning support activities.

Why we exist

Because youth-led actions create sustainable change. We train leaders, provide resources, and foster community alliances to uplift the most vulnerable.

Our Advocacies

Kalusugan (Health)

Health education, community check-ups, and sanitation programs.

Kaalaman (Education)

School supplies, learning sessions, and youth training.

Kalikasan (Environment)

Reforestation, clean-ups, and waste reduction campaigns.

Projects & Programs

Tree Planting

Annual drives to restore local watersheds and urban green spaces.

Feeding Programs

Regular community feeding events for children and families in need.

School Support

Supply drives, reading sessions, and tutorial programs.

Awareness Campaigns

Health, environmental protection, and youth civic engagement.
